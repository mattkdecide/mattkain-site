Early Observations

🏗️
Operating Model
Structure Still Taking Shape
APAC reporting lines and budget ownership are clearer than expected at market level, but the regional layer between Pete and global still needs mapping. Who actually owns the brief vs. who influences it will define how we run.
📐
Account Scope
Breadth vs Depth Tension
The APAC mandate is wide — 5+ markets, three product lines, multiple agency relationships. The risk is an inch-deep strategy across everything. First 30 days will be spent deliberately narrowing to where concentration of effort actually moves revenue.
🤝
Team & Culture
Strong Individual Talent
Early signals from Pete and Will suggest technically strong people with deep channel expertise. The opportunity is on the strategic side — connecting performance execution to brand and pipeline in a joined-up way that neither holdco relationship currently delivers fully.
🚀
Opportunity
Agency Relationships Ripe for Reset
The Zenith / Publicis relationship appears transactional at present. There's a clear opening to position this as a strategic renegotiation — not just a contract renewal. The appetite on both sides is there; the question is whether Adobe has the internal alignment to move on it.
